OWASP Threats

OWASP Top 10 is the list of the 10 most common application vulnerabilities. These are the vulnerabilities that a cybersecurity engineer/penetration tester try to mitigate. They also try to find and analyse new security threats that might have gone unnoticed in the past.

OWASP TOP 10

1.SQL Injection
2.Broken Authentication
3.Sensitive Data Exposure
4.XML External Entities(XEE)
5.Broken Acess Control
6.Security Misconfiguration
7.Cross Site Scripting(XSS)
8.Insecure Deserialization
9.Using Components with known vulnerabilities
10.Insufficient logging and monitoring
